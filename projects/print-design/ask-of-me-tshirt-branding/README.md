# ASK OF ME — T-Shirt Branding

## Project Overview
“ASK OF ME” is a faith-inspired T-shirt branding designed to communicate prayer, dependence on God, and bold belief through simple yet expressive typography.

The design is rooted in **Psalm 2:8**, emphasizing the power of asking and trusting God for divine provision and authority.

---

## Design Objective
To create a wearable design that:
- Communicates a strong biblical message
- Feels modern, bold, and expressive
- Works well for church programs, outreaches, and casual wear
- Maintains clarity and impact across different applications

---

## Design Concept
The central phrase **“ASK OF ME”** is styled in bold, hand-drawn typography to convey urgency, confidence, and faith.

Supporting design elements include:
- A speech-bubble inspired form to reinforce the idea of *speaking* and *asking*
- Emphasis strokes and accents to add energy and movement
- A scripture tag to anchor the message biblically

---

## Visual Direction
The visual style prioritizes:
- High contrast for print clarity
- Strong typography for legibility from a distance
- Balanced composition suitable for fabric application

The design system is adaptable and can be applied across different color treatments and garment variations without losing its impact.

---

## Typography Style
- Bold, custom-styled lettering for the main message
- Clean supporting text for scripture reference
- Clear hierarchy to guide visual focus

---

## Application
This design is suitable for:
- Church programs and conferences
- Evangelism and outreach events
- Christian youth gatherings
- Everyday casual wear

The branding is scalable and works effectively across different T-shirt colors and print formats.
